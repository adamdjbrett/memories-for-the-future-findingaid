The Expert Mechanism on the Rights of Indigenous Peoples (EMRIP) provides thematic studies, advice, and recommendations that support the implementation of the United Nations Declaration on the Rights of Indigenous Peoples. This page gathers statements, reports, and materials related to EMRIP sessions and outcomes.
